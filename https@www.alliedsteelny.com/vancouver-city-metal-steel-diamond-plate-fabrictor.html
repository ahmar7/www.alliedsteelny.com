Steel Diamond Plate for VBC

VBC, Rolled Carbon, Low Alloy commercial quality Steel Diamond Plate, also known as Floor plate, Tread plate, Checker plate and Safety plate, has a bluish grey finish and is one of Bayats Manufacturing of VBC most popular steel products. A four way raised pattern on one side of the steel plate that provides skid resistance for high traffic areas in Vancouver City, such as floors, ramps, stairs and marine docks. Bayats Manufacturing Ltd are Custom Steel Fabricators, we offer a myriad of different steel and metal services that cut the steel, punch steel and metal, bend and weld A786 steel diamond plate into many different products for countless industries in Vancouver City including commercial construction, transportation, truck repair, industrial maintenance, specialty, movie and entertainment. Bayats Manufacturing has a long list of fabricated products including custom metal ramps, steel walk ways, metal loading docks and trench covers as well as, metal/steel stair treads and platforms used in fire escapes and building stairwells throughout VBC five boroughs. At Bayats Manufacturing Ltd we have the capacity to fabricate much heavier diamond plate, including thicknesses of up to as thick as 1 inch. Our state of the art Plasma cutting units and Oxy-Fuel burning machine can custom fabricate metal/steel diamond plate to any shape or size, always with precision tolerances and accuracy according to your exact specifications. Bayats Manufacturing of Vancouver outperforms our competition because we are a highly reliable Steel Diamond Plate supplier as well as a quality custom steel fabricator with some of the best customer service in the steel industry.

Aluminum Diamond Plate VBC

Aluminum Diamond Plate is available in 3003 and 6061 aluminum alloy grade

Bayats Manufacturing Ltd custom fabricates 3003 Aluminum Tread plate, a general-purpose alloy grade Diamond Plate with moderate physical strength and excellent slip resistant properties that has good workability and is easily bendable and weldable but may not be desirable for machining because of its softness. Great for certain construction jobs in VBC, it is very light and visually appealing with a highly polished-bright chrome like finish. 3003 and 6061 aluminum alloy grade offers excellent corrosion resistance and is used in many architectural cosmetic applications and custom automotive accessory parts. Bayats Manufacturing of Vancouver steel warehouse/plant will custom fabricate and cut to size 3003 Aluminum Diamond Plate to any shape or design, always to your exact specification utilizing our latest Plasma cutting technology.

The 6061 Aluminum Floor Plate is a stronger alloy grade Diamond Plate/Safety plate with great machinability and durability making it an extremely popular aluminum grade. Because 6061 Aluminum Diamond plate has been heat-treated it is stronger than 3003 and more durable. Additionally 6061 has a duller brushed mill finish that's still appealing, unlike the flashier 3003 grade. This grade is best suited and often used in high traffic applications such as outdoor walkways that require skid resistance, higher strength, durability and atmospheric corrosion resistance properties

Stainless Steel for VBC –

In Vancouver City, your best place to order custom fabricated Stainless Steel Diamond Plate is Bayats Manufacturing Ltd. Stainless Steel plate from Bayats Manufacturing Ltd is available in type T-304 grade. This steel is a general-purpose commercial grade alloy possessing excellent formability and high physical strength of 34 KSI min yield strength and 85 KSI min tensile strength. T-304 Steel is used in a wide range of VBC industrial construction and renovation projects requiring corrosive resistance properties against atmospheric conditions. This metal is hot rolled; pickled and annealed giving it a dull grey mill finish, unlike the shinier finishes found on Aluminum diamond plate, it also could be special ordered to a polished finish. Custom Metal fabrication of Stainless Steel Diamond Plate is Bayats Manufacturing Ltd specialty, we possess the fabricating capabilities to cut, bend, form, punch, drill and weld providing you, our customers with superior quality Stainless Steel Diamond Plate products for any Vancouver City job. Stainless Steel Diamond Plate is used in many different Vancouver based industries such as industrial maintenance and marine construction. Bayats Manufacturing Ltd fabricated Diamond Steel products are most often used in VBC for steel walkways, steel ramps, diamond plate stair treads, steel loading docks, metal catwalks and floors. T304 steel diamond plate grade offers superior strength, durable wear, improved traction and high-level corrosion resistance properties, its very desirable steel product that is utilized by VBC contractors.
